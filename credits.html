Data Sources & Licenses

Tsubu's vocabulary, examples, and pronunciation are built on excellent open language resources. We gratefully acknowledge the following, used under their respective licenses.

JMdict / EDICT — dictionary data

© Electronic Dictionary Research and Development Group (EDRDG) · Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-SA 4.0)

Japanese word entries and readings derive from the JMdict project. Project · Licence

Tatoeba — example sentences

© Tatoeba contributors · Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY)

Some example sentences are sourced from the Tatoeba corpus. tatoeba.org

Kuromoji — reading annotations (furigana)

© Atilika Inc. and contributors · Apache License 2.0

Furigana / reading segmentation is generated with the Kuromoji Japanese morphological analyzer. atilika.org

Tanos — JLPT level references

© Jonathan Waller

JLPT level groupings reference vocabulary and grammar lists compiled by Tanos. tanos.co.uk/jlpt

Google Cloud Text-to-Speech — pronunciation audio

Audio synthesized with Google Cloud TTS (Neural2 voices), used under the Google Cloud Platform terms.

Word and example-sentence pronunciation is pre-generated and bundled with the app.

Meanings (Chinese / English translations) and many curated examples and question items are original to Tsubu. None of the above sources involve transmitting any user data.
